History: TechCon SoCal emerged as the premier innovation conference, co-organized by multiple influential organizations, including Tech Coast Angels, UCSD, TIE, OPEN, SDVG, IGNITE, and Connect. Its roots trace back to a collective vision: to bring together the brightest minds in technology, investments, academia, and research. Since its inception, TechCon has been a driving force, shaping the future of innovation.
Mission: TechCon’s mission extends beyond financial support. It aspires to inspire the next generation of entrepreneurs by fostering an ecosystem where innovation thrives, investments abound, entrepreneurship flourishes, and positive change permeates every aspect of society.
Vision: Through a blend of insightful keynotes, dynamic panel discussions, and interactive case studies, TechCon paints the vision of a connected and autonomous future. It’s about answering questions that have deep strategic and tactical significance, backed by data and insights. The conference aims to remain accessible to all, celebrating technical achievements while addressing challenges head-on.
Past speakers include:
  1. Dr. Maya Patel: A trailblazer in artificial intelligence, Dr. Patel has revolutionized personalized medicine. Her keynote will explore how AI can transform healthcare, from remote care to preventive solutions.
  2. Elijah Wong:As the founder of Quantum Innovations, Wong has disrupted the semiconductor industry. His session will delve into secure next-gen chips, powering the tech landscape beyond imagination.
